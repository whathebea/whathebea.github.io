E-commerce website - Planning Phase

2022-10-24
1 minute read
tech
angular , java , spring , mysql

Hello everyone! Today I spent most of my day studying for AZ-900, I am following a learning path my company offers so that when the time comes I can take the exam and get certified. I am also thinking of creating a project using all the technologies I am currently studying: Angular, Spring, MySQL. I am mostly still planning it, I think building an e-commerce application will be very useful to showcase my skills.

I really want to make something that challenges me and that I can be proud of so here is how I am planning to structure the whole project:

Admin Panel

  • Register products (and categories + brands)
  • Register users
  • Login
  • List all the orders
  • List all the registered users

Store

  • List the available products
  • Filter products by category and brand
  • Shopping Cart
  • Client Login and Signup
  • Change personal information
  • Listing the orders

I know it’s not much but that’s what I have for today. I am going to start coding now and hopefully will have something to show tomorrow!
